Scout

Offense
  • Health: 125 HP
  • Abilities: Double Jump, Fast Movement
  • Primary Weapon: Scattergun

Overview

The Scout is the fastest class in Team Fortress 2, specializing in hit-and-run tactics and objective capturing. His double jump ability allows him to reach places other classes cannot, making him ideal for flanking and quick escapes.

Background

Hailing from Boston, the Scout is known for his cocky attitude and relentless energy. He excels at harassing enemies and is often the first to reach control points or intelligence briefcases.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use double jump to dodge enemy fire and reach high ground.
  • Capture objectives quickly thanks to the Scout's speed bonus.
  • Flank slower classes and avoid direct confrontations with Heavies or sentries.

Trivia

  • The Scout is voiced by Nathan Vetterlein.
  • He is the only class that can double jump.
  • His Boston accent and fast-talking style are iconic in TF2.
